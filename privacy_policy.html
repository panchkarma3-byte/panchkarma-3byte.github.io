Privacy Policy for Panchakarma Wellness

Last Updated: September 21, 2025

Welcome to Panchakarma Wellness ("we," "our," "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web platform. Our platform is designed to provide a comprehensive digital experience for managing Panchakarma therapy.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use our service.

A. Information You Provide to Us:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect personal information such as your full name, email address, phone number, password, and your role (Patient or Practitioner).
  • Practitioner Profile Information: Practitioners can provide additional professional details, including their specialties and a base session price.
  • Third-Party Login: If you register or sign in using Google, we will receive personal information from that service as permitted by your privacy settings on that service.
  • Payment Information: To process payments for therapy sessions, we use a third-party payment gateway, Razorpay. We do not store your full credit card or bank account details.

B. Information Collected Automatically:

  • Session and Usage Data: Like most websites, we may collect information about how you access and use the platform, including session information to keep you logged in.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platform.
  • Manage your account and user profile.
  • Process appointment requests and automate therapy scheduling.
  • Send you personalized notifications and reminders for your therapy.
  • Facilitate communication between patients and practitioners.
  • Process transactions and manage payment collection through Razorpay.
  • Monitor and analyze usage to improve the user experience.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with the following third parties:

  • With Other Users: We share information between patients and practitioners as necessary to schedule and conduct therapy sessions.
  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors that help us operate our platform, including:
    • Firebase: For database management and user authentication services.
    • Razorpay: For processing payments securely.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law.

4. Data Security and Compliance

We are committed to protecting your data. Our platform is built on a secure technical stack, using services like Firebase which provides robust, managed security for authentication and data.

Our data handling practices are designed in alignment with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), 2023. During registration, we require your agreement to this policy via a mandatory consent checkbox to ensure your data is handled transparently and lawfully.

5. Your Rights and Choices

In accordance with the DPDP Act, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Correction: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

You can manage your profile information from your dashboard. For other requests, please contact us.
